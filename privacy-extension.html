Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

Piggy Bong Chrome Extension is built with privacy at its core. We believe your shopping data is yours and yours alone. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

Data Collection

The extension operates entirely on your device using:

  • On-Device AI Processing: All analysis is performed locally using Google's Gemini Nano AI, which runs directly in your browser
  • No External Servers: Your shopping cart data never leaves your device
  • No Tracking: We don't track your browsing history, purchases, or behavior
  • No Cookies: We don't set any tracking cookies

How the Extension Works

When you use Piggy Bong:

  • You visit a K-pop shopping site (like Ktown4u or Weverse)
  • You click the Piggy Bong extension button
  • The extension reads your shopping cart data from the current page
  • Gemini Nano (running in your browser) analyzes the items locally
  • Priority rankings are displayed to you
  • All processing happens on your device - nothing is sent anywhere

Permissions

The extension requires the following permissions:

  • Active Tab: To read shopping cart data from the page you're viewing
  • Storage: To save your preferences (bias, budget) locally on your device

These permissions are used solely for the extension's functionality and never to collect data.

Third-Party Services

Gemini Nano: The AI model runs entirely in your Chrome browser. Google does not receive your shopping data through this extension.

We do not integrate with any analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Security

Since all processing happens locally on your device and we don't collect or transmit data, your information is as secure as your own device.

Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. We will notify users of any material changes through the extension's update notes.
