2. Description of Service

Piggy Bong is a free Chrome Extension that provides AI-powered shopping recommendations for K-pop merchandise. The Extension:

  • Analyzes items in your shopping cart on supported websites
  • Uses on-device AI (Google Gemini Nano) to generate priority rankings
  • Provides personalized shopping insights based on your preferences
  • Operates entirely on your local device with no data collection
